Tetris In A Day

Details

This project was intended to be a fun way to play around with the bevy game engine. My buddy WolfenGames and I worked on this over the span of a day and crammed as many Tetris features in as we could in that time.

Take Aways

Overall i was really happy with this. It may not have been the prettiest thing in the world but it worked, and I was really happy with how clean we managed to keep the code considering the rushed nature of the project.
